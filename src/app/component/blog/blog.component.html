Teleport Technologies PLC is a prominent technology company based in Ethiopia, focusing on digital solutions and services.





Here are some key aspects of the company:

Overview
Teleport Technologies PLC is a multi-unit technology company that aims to address fundamental problems through technology. They focus on maximizing business efficiency and enabling businesses to join the digital economy.

Services and Solutions
Teleport Technologies offers a range of services, including:
  • Digital Airtime Distribution: They are the first and largest digital airtime distribution service provider in Ethiopia.
  • Virtual Distribution and Logistics Platforms: These platforms facilitate service delivery for both individual and enterprise clients.
  • Payment Platforms: They provide customer-centric payment solutions designed to simplify services and reduce costs.


Mission and Vision
Mission: To empower people through technology, simplify services, and drive down costs.
Vision: To digitally transform value distribution for inclusive access, sustainability, and scale.

Partnerships
Teleport Technologies has established partnerships with various enterprises, including mobile operators, financial institutions, and other B2C enterprises. These partnerships help expand their scale and scope.

Achievements
Revolutionizing Airtime Distribution: They have significantly improved the way airtime is distributed in Ethiopia, contributing to environmental betterment and reducing foreign currency spending on printed voucher cards.
In-house Expertise: All their software solutions are developed in-house, ensuring high quality and tailored solutions for their clients.
